OUR PROGRAM

Our 8-month program is divided into 14 modules. After the program, students join a 4-week intensive bootcamp to work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at BeCentral (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week,students are supported online in their self-study,homework and peer learning.
